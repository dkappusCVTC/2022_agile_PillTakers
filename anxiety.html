Anxiety/Depression

List of Medication for Anxiety:

Anxiety or anxiety disorders are an umbrella term for a group of mental illnesses loosely associated with each other based on the symptoms they cause, usually manifesting in one or more of the following symptoms:

  • Panic/fear/uneasiness
  • Insomnia or issues sleeping
  • Inability to remain still or calm
  • Sensations in feet or hands including cold, sweat, numbness or tingling
  • Trouble breathing
  • Heart Issues
  • Nausea or Vomiting
  • Dizziness
  • Muscle Tension
  • Dry Mouth

Common anxiety disorders include panic disorder, depression, social anxiety disorder, specific phobias, post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD) and generalized anxiety disorder.

Antidepressant Medications for Anxiety

The first line of defense against anxiety is usually an antidepressant drug. Antidepressant drugs are taken long term as they act on and try to restore the balance of serotonin and norepinephrine chemicals in the brain.

Here is a list of anti-depression medications:

  • Celexa - off label medication for panic disorder, social phobia and trichotillomania
  • Cymbalta - prescribed for generalized anxiety
  • Lexapro - prescribed for generalized anxiety
  • Prozac - prescribed for obsessive compulsive disorder (OCD) and panic disorder
  • Paxil - prescribed for OCD, panic disorder, social phobia, GAL and post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)
  • Zoloft - prescribed for panic disorder, PTSD, social phobia and OCD
  • Trazodone - off label medication for for panic disorders
  • Effexor XR - prescribed to adults general & anxiety, and panic disorder

Monoamine Oxidase Inhibitors (MOAI’s)

MAOI’s are another older and powerful class of antidepressants used to treat anxiety. Monoamine oxidase inhibitors are not first choice treatments as improved options are available, but may be useful where other treatments have failed.

  • Marplan - off label drug for social phobia
  • Nardil - off label drug for panic disorders and social phobia
  • Emsam - off label drug for social phobia
  • Parnate - off label drug for social phobia

Beta-Blocker Medications for Anxiety

These medications are antihypertensive drugs because of their use in treating high blood pressure. Beta-blockers and other similar medications may reduce the physical symptoms of anxiety.

  • Tenormin - off label drug used for situational/performance anxiety
  • Corgard - off label drug used for situational/performance anxiety
  • Propranolol - off label drug for panic disorder, situational/performance anxiety, PTSD and anxiety in general

Antipsychotic Medications List Used To Treat Anxiety

Antipsychotic drugs are often prescribed together with other medications used to treat anxiety. These drugs are prescribed when other treatments have’t worked due to having more severe side effects.

Here is a the list of antisychotic medications:

  • Zyprexa - off label drug used for general anxiety
  • Seroquel - effectiveness in treating anxiety and safety with other medication still being researched, pending FDA-approval for general anxiety disorder
  • Risperdal - off label drug use for anxiety (in general
